To the best mom in the world

Mason's Reflections on Mom

By Mason, age 13  ·  Mother's Day 2026

The first thing that pops into my head when I think of Mom…

"How much I love her."

🌸

What I See In You

Something you do that you don't realize I notice…

She works extremely hard to make all of us happy. And I really appreciate it. She makes a lot of sacrifices for us, for the little things, and the big things.

A sacrifice I appreciate more now than ever…

How hard she works at her job. I know she doesn't enjoy it, but she does it to support the family. And I really do appreciate it.

Something I'm proud of you for getting through…

Work. Dealing with rude people and sick patients. I know it's not easy.

Something I wish you did more for yourself…

I wish she spent more time doing what she loves. I love seeing mom happy.

✨

What You've Taught Me

The best advice you've ever given me…

Mom has told me to stand up for what I believe in no matter what. I will take that advice on my life journey with me.

When I have kids someday, I want to raise them the way you raised me…

I'd like to teach my kids, like how she raised me, to always stand up for yourself. And to never let someone else decide who you are. No one gets to make that decision but you.

Something I got from you…

My political views. I learn a lot from mom.

A way we're similar…

We both have pride in our country and beliefs. We are not scared to show the world what we believe in.

Something you say that I'll catch myself saying someday…

"I love you to the moon and back."

💝

Only You

What you're genuinely the best at…

Puzzles. She is amazing at puzzles.

Your signature thing…

Organization. Mom loves to be organized.

Something I get now that I didn't as a kid…

Puzzles. I wouldn't think puzzles were so fun when I was little. But now, I enjoy puzzles.

What you love about being my mom…

She loves that I'm polite to people.

What even strangers notice about you…

Ava said that my mom was really nice.

How we're different (with a $100 on the line)…

Mom is convinced I'll go to law school. I've always wanted MIT. We have a $100 bet on it.

😂 The funniest thing you've ever done…

When we were at a boardwalk, we passed by a stand with two parrots. Mom was so afraid of the parrots that she went completely out of the walking path to avoid them.

🌿

Moments I'll Never Forget

A moment you were there for me that I'll always remember…

Whenever I get hurt. When I got stung by a jellyfish, or when I was in the hospital, she always wanted me to get better, and she helped me power through those times.

What I want you to know that I don't usually say…

She is the perfect mom. I couldn't ask for anyone better. She does the mom stuff right.

Mom, the thing I want you to know is…

"…that I'm proud of you. Whatever I accomplish in life, it will be because of you — it will be from what you have taught me."

With all my love, Mason ❤️
